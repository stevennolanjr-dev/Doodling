2027 marks a capability threshold, not an invasion deadline.

Executive Assessment

The "Davidson Window" refers to U.S. assessments that the PLA could be capable of Taiwan contingencies by 2027. Two major exercises in 2025—Strait Thunder-2025A (April) and Justice Mission 2025 (December)—validate this timeline and reveal doctrinal evolution.

Intel Update Justice Mission 2025 (Dec 29-30)

🛡️ CCG as Blockade Enforcement Mechanism

Justice Mission 2025 deployed 15 CCG vessels—a record. For the first time, CCG patrolled around Pratas Island during a blockade exercise. CCG ships entered Taiwan's prohibited waters around Wuqiu (within 1.6nm) and Matsu (within 1.3nm). CCG practiced "interception and detention operations"—the enforcement mechanism for a legal-warfare blockade. The CCG operated together with PLAN ships, suggesting coordination for a two-tier blockade: CCG interdicts civilian vessels (under "law enforcement" pretext) while PLAN enforces anti-access against military forces.

🚢 The "Invisible Fleet" Problem

Why traditional warning indicators will miss the signal

For two decades, DoD assessed invasion as unlikely because the PLAN lacked sufficient amphibious lift. This was a capabilities-based analysis that counted grey hulls. The correct framework is legal and mobilization-based analysis: the PLA solved its "hardware gap" with a "software solution"—legal mobilization of the civilian fleet.

Justice Mission 2025 Target Profile

Blockade Target Selection

Justice Mission 2025 positioned forces at Taiwan's key ports: Keelung (north, adjacent to Taipei), Kaohsiung (south, largest port, energy imports), and Hualien (east, intervention blocking). PRC state media explicitly mentioned "cutting off power" as an exercise objective. The exercise zones covered Taiwan Strait approaches, cutting routes to Kinmen and Matsu.

Policy Implication

Deterrence requires denying PLA confidence in a quick, low-cost victory. The most likely failure mode is not a bolt-from-the-blue invasion, but a slow squeeze—gray zone to CCG blockade to kinetic coercion to fait accompli—where each step is calibrated to stay below the threshold of U.S. kinetic response. Justice Mission 2025 showed the PLA is now practicing the transition from blockade to assault—the squeeze may be faster than previously assessed. Watch for CCG "law enforcement" escalation, Type 075 deployment, and civilian RORO diversions.
